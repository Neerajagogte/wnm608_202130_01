Cuppa Noodles

STEP 1: Tear half of the cup's lid
STEP 2: Pour boiling water and close lid
STEP 3: Wait for 4 to 5 minutes
STEP 4: Eat!

Pizza

STEP 1: Heat a pan on the stove
STEP 2: Place your leftover pizza on the pan until it reaches desired crispness
STEP 3: Turn off the stove and eat!

Toast

STEP 1: Place the bread slices in the toaster
STEP 2: Select temperature and press the lever
STEP 3: Once the bread pops up, place it in a plate, apply butter generously
and have it with a simmering cup of tea!
